Refund Policy

At Swift Node Web, we strive to provide exceptional hosting services and customer satisfaction. Our refund policy is designed to be fair and transparent. Please review the following terms regarding refunds and cancellations.

1. Eligibility for Refunds

We offer refunds under the following circumstances:

  • New Customers: If you are unsatisfied with our services, you may request a refund within the first 30 days of your initial purchase.
  • Technical Issues: If we are unable to resolve technical issues that affect the core functionality of our services, you may be eligible for a refund.

2. Non-Refundable Services

The following services are non-refundable:

  • Domain registration, transfer, or renewal fees.
  • SSL certificate purchases.
  • Add-ons and custom services (e.g., website migration, setup fees).
  • Payments made for services exceeding the refund eligibility period.

3. Refund Process

  • To request a refund, contact our support team via email at swiftnode@gmail.com or through the contact form on our website.
  • Include your account details, the service in question, and the reason for your refund request.
  • Once approved, refunds will be processed within 7-10 business days via the original payment method.

4. Cancellations

  • Services can be canceled at any time through your customer account portal.
  • If the cancellation is made outside the refund eligibility period, you will not be eligible for a refund.

5. Exclusions and Limitations

  • Refunds are not granted for violations of our Terms and Conditions.
  • Refunds may not be provided for promotional or discounted services unless otherwise stated.
  • Multiple refund requests from the same user may result in service suspension.

6. Amendments to the Refund Policy

We reserve the right to update or modify this refund policy at any time without prior notice. Changes will be effective upon posting on our website.
